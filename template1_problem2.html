Template 1 — Sine / Cosine (Problem 2)

Given:
tan A = 3/4
→
Find:
sin A and cos A
Step 1: tan A = opposite/adjacent = 3/4. Let opposite=3k, adjacent=4k.
Step 2: hypotenuse = √(3k²+4k²) = 5k.
Step 3: sin A = opposite/hypotenuse = (3k)/(5k) = 3/5 = 0.6
Step 4: cos A = adjacent/hypotenuse = (4k)/(5k) = 4/5 = 0.8
Answer: sin A = 3/5, cos A = 4/5
